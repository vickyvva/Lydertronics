About Lydertronics

Precision Data for Intelligent Systems

Lydertronics is a specialized AI data services company operating at trainbot.in, dedicated to building the training foundations that modern ML and robotics systems depend on.

We combine deep domain expertise in computer vision, speech processing, and robotic motion analysis with rigorous quality control to deliver datasets that make AI systems smarter, faster, and more reliable.

From early-stage AI startups to advanced robotics research labs across the USA, Europe, and India — we are the data backbone that powers breakthroughs.

🌐 Domain: trainbot.in

Serving AI companies, robotics startups, research institutions, and technology firms globally with enterprise-grade annotation services.

What We Do

End-to-End AI Data Services

Comprehensive annotation and dataset preparation across images, video, audio, and robotic systems.

🖼️

Image Annotation

Precise pixel-level annotation for classification, segmentation, and detection tasks. Optimized for large-scale AI training pipelines.

📦

Bounding Box Annotation

Accurate 2D and 3D bounding box labeling for object detection models in autonomous vehicles, drones, and industrial vision systems.

🏷️

Image Labeling

Multi-class tagging and classification labeling for image datasets. Scalable workflows with rigorous inter-annotator agreement checks.

🎬

Video Annotation

Frame-by-frame video annotation including object tracking, action recognition, and temporal segmentation for dynamic scene understanding.

🤖

Robotic Movement Analysis

Behavioral evaluation of robotic systems — grasping accuracy, path tracking, manipulation performance, and edge-case failure tagging.

🎤

Audio Transcription

High-accuracy speech-to-text transcription across multiple languages including English and Telugu for ASR and NLP model training.

🗄️

AI Dataset Preparation

End-to-end dataset curation: collection, cleaning, formatting, splitting, and validation in ML-ready formats like COCO, YOLO, and custom schemas.
